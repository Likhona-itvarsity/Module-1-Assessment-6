Mascara

Mascara is a cosmetic commonly used to enhance the upper and lower eyelashes. It is used to darken, thicken, and define eyelashes. Normally in one of three forms-liquid, powder, or cream- the morden mascara product has various formulas; however,most contain the same basic components of pigments, oil, waxes, and preservatives. The most common form of mascara is a liquid in a tube with an application brush.
